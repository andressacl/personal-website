## Research Scope

This website documents ongoing PhD research in astroparticle physics, with a focus on:

- Cosmic-ray propagation and composition
- Multi-messenger signatures
- Numerical simulations and modeling
- Reproducible analysis workflows

The content is intended to be **educational, technical, and archival**.
